I see you want to learn more about moi.

I am a Manhattan-born, Jersey-raised, Dominican gal. Currently, I am a Senior at the University of Pennsylvania studying Digital Media Design—an interdisciplinary program that combines computer science engineering and fine arts.

I enjoy coding, especially for the web. I find it fascinating how one person (or team) can create a single application, network, or site that millions of people can access and use with a single click.
Let's talk about the artist.

I am quite the graphic design and typography junkie. I constantly asky myself why. I feel every element should have an intrinsic purpose beyond being aesthetically pleasing.

Aside from coding and designing, I love music! I play the flute and am part of Penn's Latin dance troupe, Onda Latina.

Now that you know more about me, how about you tell me a little bit about yourself? Looking forward to hearing from you!
